** How to info of package?

#+BEGIN_SRC bat
winget show PACKAGE_NAME_OR_ID
#+END_SRC

** How to do head, tail in window
`gc` refers to get content.
#+BEGIN_SRC bat
gc log.txt -head 10
gc log.txt -tail 10
gc log.txt -tail 10 -wait # equivalent to tail -f
#+END_SRC

** How to flush/clear DNS
#+BEGIN_SRC sh
ipconfig /flushdns
#+END_SRC
